What do those words mean?

Serpent
a large snake; a sly or treacherous person, especially one who exploits a position of trust in order to betray it (коварный человек)
Damnation
(in Christian belief) condemnation to eternal punishment in hell (проклятие, осуждение)
Discretion
the freedom to decide what should be done in a particular situation. (осмотрительность, благоразумие)
Retribution
punishment inflicted on someone as vengeance for a wrong or criminal act (возмездие, воздаяние)
Consequence
a result or effect of an action or condition; importance or relevance; (последствие, заключение)
Abuse
use (something) to bad effect or for a bad purpose; treat (a person or an animal) with cruelty or violence, especially regularly or repeatedly; (злоупотреблять)
Relieve
cause (pain, distress, or difficulty) to become less severe or serious; take (a burden) from someone; (облегчать, освобождать)
Intention
a thing intended; an aim or plan. the healing process of a wound.(in medicine) (намерение,цель)
Perception
the ability to see, hear, or become aware of something through the senses (восприятие, ощущение, понимание)
